4. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage (Primary):

All your journal entries, voice recordings, and personal data are stored locally on your device using Core Data with encryption. This data never leaves your device except when explicitly sent to AI processing APIs.

No Cloud Backup:

InnerBloom does not use iCloud or any cloud backup services. Your data remains exclusively on your device. If you delete the app or lose your device, your data cannot be recovered.

Important: We strongly recommend manually backing up your device using iTunes or Finder if you wish to preserve your InnerBloom data.

AI Processing (Temporary):

When you create a reflection:

  • Your voice recording is sent to third-party AI services for transcription
  • The transcription is processed by AI services to generate insights and responses
  • The specific AI model used may vary based on availability, performance, and subscription tier
  • These services process your data temporarily and do not store it permanently under enterprise agreements
  • We use enterprise-grade APIs with data processing agreements that prohibit training on your data
  • Your voice recordings are processed transiently and not retained after transcription

Security Measures:

  • Your data is protected by iOS's built-in encryption and secure sandbox environment
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all API communications
  • No central database storing your journal entries
  • iOS security model isolates app data from other apps
  • Secure authentication for API requests
  • Regular security updates
